About Us

A Platform for Young Innovators

Innovation Hub at Pilikula Regional Science Centre is a dedicated space where students from schools and colleges can explore the frontiers of science and technology. We believe every student has the potential to innovate.

Our hub provides access to cutting-edge equipment, expert mentors, and collaborative workspaces where ideas become tangible projects. From building robots to conducting experiments, the Innovation Hub nurtures curiosity and transforms it into real-world solutions.
